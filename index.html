Meet Jaskaran — Our Founder & Future Doctor

Our Mission

Education should empower, not bankrupt

"I watched talented classmates struggle not because they weren't smart enough, but because they couldn't afford the tutoring that others had. That's when I knew something had to change."

It started in Year 11, watching friends stress over exams while their wealthy classmates seemed to coast through with expensive tutors and private coaching. The gap wasn't about intelligence — it was about access.

I began sharing study techniques that worked for me: active recall, spaced repetition, effective note-taking. What started as informal study sessions turned into an online community which is now growing, connecting students who want to learn smarter, not harder.

The mission has always been the same: make quality education accessible to everyone. Not free handouts, but affordable mentoring paired with valuable free resources. Real guidance without the ridiculous price tags.

Today, Bridge The Gap Edu combines affordable 1-on-1 mentoring with free study resources and a supportive community. Success shouldn't require your parents to spend thousands — just your commitment to learning the right way.
